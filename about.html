About Konka

Konka Social Club & Restaurant is a unique and stylish venue for all occasions. We offer a range of functions, from birthday parties to large events. Our menu features a variety of delicious dishes and our bar serves a selection of refreshing beverages. Whether you're looking for a place to relax with friends or to host a special occasion, Konka is the perfect choice.

LOCATION

Konka is located at Modjaji Street, Pimville, Zone 7, Soweto. We are easily accessible by car or public transportation and have ample parking available. Come visit us today and experience all that Konka has to offer!

Functions

Konka is the perfect venue for all types of functions. Whether you're looking to host a corporate event, a wedding, a birthday party, or any other special occasion, we have the facilities and expertise to make it a success. Our dedicated team will work with you to plan and execute the perfect event, ensuring that every detail is taken care of. Contact us today to start planning your dream function at Konka.
